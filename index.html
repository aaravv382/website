Hi!

Established by the Superhouse Foundation, Allenhouse Public School located in Panki, Kanpur is one of the most reputed educational institutions of Uttar Pradesh. The institution is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary education(CBSE), and English is the medium of instruction. The Superhouse Foundation started its journey in the year 1996 with a mission to provide high-quality education and instil best social and moral values in its students. The group aims to develop strong characters of children with critical thinking skills so that they can put back in society and build a progressive world. The school also intends to develop the inherent potential of all students to become self-driven, life-long learners and caring, ethical citizens of the world. The management committee ensures all the students develop a thirst for learning and inculcate in them a jest to excel in all aspects of life. The school is furnished with well-equipped science labs, an air-conditioned school library, swimming pool, basketball court, robotic lab, indoor games rooms, a skating ring, music room, infirmary, air-conditioned classrooms, multipurpose halls, a covered play area, a mini amphitheatre, a dedicated yoga hall and many others. Extracurricular activities play a vital role in shaping the lives of students which is why there are separate rooms for Western, Indian classical vocal and instrumental music, art and crafts as well as dance. The school also offers facilities for learning Urdu, French and German. The school organizes regular inter-school events and concerts where the students participate and bring forth their hidden talent. Since its establishment, the group has been aspiring to set new standards in the various fields of education - be it basic or higher professional learning. The school has adopted, adapted the proven pedagogies and administrative system to kindle a spirit of nobility in the young minds and constantly strive to set new benchmarks in the field of education.
